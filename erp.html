ERP Overview

SJVN's ERP integrates Finance, Human Resources, and Inventory Management, streamlining operations, improving data accuracy, and enabling efficient decision-making across all departments.

Finance

Finance Module Overview

Manages financial operations, budgeting, accounting, and reporting. Supports compliance and financial decisions.

Features:
  • Budget planning and allocation
  • Financial reporting and compliance
  • Accounts payable & receivable
  • Expense tracking & forecasting
HR

HR Module Overview

Automates employee management, payroll, recruitment, and performance tracking.

Features:
  • Employee database management
  • Payroll and benefits administration
  • Recruitment workflow automation
  • Performance evaluation and appraisals
  • Training & development tracking
Inventory

Inventory Module Overview

Tracks stock levels, manages procurement, and monitors supply chains for smooth operations.

Features:
  • Real-time stock monitoring
  • Procurement & supplier management
  • Warehouse and location tracking
  • Supply chain optimization
  • Automated reorder alerts
