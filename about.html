Company Overview

SJVN Limited, a Mini Ratna Category-I enterprise under the Ministry of Power, Government of India, is engaged in hydroelectric power generation and transmission. Established in 1988, SJVN has contributed significantly to the energy sector in India and neighboring countries, with a vision to provide sustainable and eco-friendly power solutions.

Over the years, SJVN has diversified into other renewable energy sources, including solar and wind, maintaining its commitment to green energy initiatives. The company has also been active in CSR activities, contributing to the socio-economic development of communities around its project sites.

History

SJVN was established in 1988 to harness the hydroelectric potential of the Satluj river. The company has successfully executed several large-scale projects, contributing to India’s growing energy needs.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to provide sustainable and eco-friendly power solutions, ensuring reliable electricity for future generations.

Our mission is to maintain operational excellence, pursue renewable energy innovations, and contribute to national development through responsible corporate practices.

Leadership

Meet the Board of Directors and CMD who guide SJVN with strategic vision and operational expertise. The leadership team emphasizes transparency, innovation, and sustainability in all company operations.

CSR / Sustainability

SJVN actively participates in Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives, including education, healthcare, and community development programs near its project sites. Sustainability remains a core principle in all projects.

Awards & Recognitions

SJVN has been recognized with multiple national awards for operational excellence, renewable energy initiatives, and corporate governance.
